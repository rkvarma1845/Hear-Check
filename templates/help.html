Hearing Assessment Application - Help Page

Introduction

Welcome to the Hearing Assessment Application! This tool helps you evaluate your hearing by playing a sound at various volumes and recording your ability to hear it. Follow the instructions below to complete the assessment.

How to Use the Application

  1. Start the Hearing Assessment

    On the homepage, click the "Start Hearing Assessment" button. You will be directed to the assessment page.

  2. Enter Your Age

    Once on the assessment page, you will be asked to provide your age. This helps us record your hearing results accurately.

  3. Play the Sound

    After entering your age, an audio player will appear. You can click the Play button to start the sound. The default volume of the sound is 10%.

  4. Adjust the Volume (Optional)

    You can adjust the volume using the Increase Volume and Decrease Volume buttons. This allows you to manually change the volume if needed. The volume will be displayed as a percentage, ranging from 0% to 100%.

  5. Respond to the Sound

    Once you have heard the sound, respond by clicking either:

    • Yes, I can hear – If you can hear the sound at the current volume.
    • No, I cannot hear – If you cannot hear the sound.
  6. Automatic Volume Increase

    If you cannot hear the sound and the volume is below 100%, the application will automatically increase the volume by 10%. The process will repeat until you either hear the sound or the volume reaches 100%.

  7. View Your Results

    If you can hear the sound and click Yes, you will be shown a message confirming that you can hear the sound. The volume and your age will be recorded. If the volume reaches 100% and you still cannot hear the sound, the application will suggest consulting a hearing specialist.

Features

  • Volume Control: Manually adjust the sound volume using the increase and decrease buttons, or the volume will adjust automatically if you cannot hear.
  • Audio Player: Integrated audio player with controls for playing and pausing the sound.
  • Simple User Interface: A clean and intuitive design for ease of use.
  • Results Page: After completing the assessment, you’ll receive feedback based on your responses.

Troubleshooting

  • I can't hear the sound even at 100% volume:

    If you cannot hear the sound at full volume, it is advised that you consult a hearing professional for further assistance.

  • Audio not playing:

    Ensure your device is not muted, and your speakers or headphones are working correctly.
